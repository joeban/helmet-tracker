About Virginia Tech STAR Testing

Virginia Tech's STAR (Summation of Tests for the Analysis of Risk) methodology provides the most comprehensive bicycle helmet safety evaluation available. Unlike basic pass/fail safety certifications, STAR testing simulates real-world cycling accidents and provides nuanced safety scores that allow meaningful comparisons between helmet models.

Lower STAR scores indicate better protection. A helmet with a score of 8.0 provides significantly better protection than one with a score of 15.0.
